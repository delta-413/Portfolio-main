About Me!

Hawoo! My name is Delta, and I'm an aspiring something! My self proclaimed title used to be aspiring programmer, but I'm not sure what I want to do, I still know a good amount of web design though. Programmer or not, I am eager to learn more, so expect more little projects to show up here over time! As of now there isn't many projects because my motivation to make stuff varies so much. Scroll down to see my previous works, and education about practical(and slightly less pratical but still creative) skills that I have!

Interests

I first got interested in the computer science field when I was little and saw those movies where a hacker was typing away at light speed with green text scrolling through the screen. Of course that's not how coding works at all(although I wished it did lol).

Although I'm only knowledgeable in web pages, I still have lots of fun doing it! I participated in an extracurricular activity called TSA, Technology Student Association. It is a club that is about all things STEM. Most importantly, it allowed me to use my web design skills outside of school while I attended the club.

I also really like music, although I know next to nothing about it(I've only taken one class of music theory and still can't play any instruments) I still wanna learn more about it!

Slightly more related to coding, I've taken a video editing class and I enjoyed it! I loved collaberating with my friends when working on editing, it really made it so much funnier and memorable. I never thought I would like video editing before taking that class, but I'm very glad I did take that class. I've learned to use Adobe Products like Photoshop and Premiere Pro in my video editing class.

Last but not least I'm REALLY into creating custom minecraft worlds using a program called WorldPainter. I absolutely love using WorldPainter, and I'm very eager to share what I've made with it! Expect more worlds to show up here because I'm not gonna stop making these!

Education

I have successfully completed a 3 year course of web design in high school. Before that, I've taken a Computer Science Pricipals class to get me started on my coding journey. My 3 year course of Web Design taught Html, Css, and Javascript!

I've also taken a video editing class and music theory class like I've mentioned, and my WorldPainter knowledge was mostly self taught after watching a few initial youtube tutorials.

Every Worldpainter project has this goal in mind: to learn how to use a feature I haven't used yet in that program. This makes every world I make fun and educational. The best part is seeing how far I've come by looking back on my first worlds compared to my most recent worlds.
